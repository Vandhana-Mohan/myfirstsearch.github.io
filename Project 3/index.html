How to Play !!!

  • Type or select a number between 1 - 100
  • Click Check to find if its correct.
  • If your guess is too low or too high, you'll get a hint.
  • You have 10 attempts to find the number.
  • Dont forget - Keep Smiling 😍
X

How to Score !!!

  • If you find the number in first attempt you win 10 points.
  • If you find in 2nd,3rd, 4th....8th, 9th attempt, you will win 9,8,7,..3,2 points     
  • If you find in the last attempt you win 1 point.
  • If you dont find in 10 attempts, you will score 0 points
  • High Score records your highest score.
  • Best Wishes 💗
